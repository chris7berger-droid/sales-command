## Top 3 Urgent

1. **C2 — Gate `check-orphan-users` immediately (CRITICAL).** `supabase/functions/check-orphan-users/index.ts:8` still runs unauth: a single `curl` is enough to invoke service-role `get_orphan_auth_user_count()` and trigger a Resend email per request. With no `verify_jwt` entry in `supabase/config.toml`, the platform doesn't gate it either. Fix: add `[functions.check-orphan-users] verify_jwt = true` to config.toml AND require a timing-safe `CRON_SECRET` header at the top of the handler; call from a scheduled service-role job only.
2. **C1 / A1 (Pass C) — Drop `.vercel.app` substring CORS allowance across all 20 money/admin functions.** Anyone can register `*.vercel.app` and host JS that posts credentialed requests against `send-invoice`, `qb-*`, `stripe-webhook`, `invite-user`, `reset-password`, etc. — the browser attaches the logged-in admin's JWT, and CORS echoes the attacker origin. Every "authenticated" protection on these endpoints becomes CSRF-bypassable. Persistent since 2026-05-17. Fix: replace `origin.endsWith(".vercel.app")` with an exact-host allowlist; deliver preview-env tokens server-side, not via origin matching.
3. **B-anon-deleted-at sweep + B4/B5 unscoped public RPCs.** `proposals_public_sign_token`, `invoices_public_view_token`, and the corresponding child policies (`proposal_wtc`, `call_log`, `customers`) still don't filter `deleted_at IS NULL` (`supabase/migrations/20260510120000_signing_token_expiry_and_consume.sql:201,212,228` + `20260520095000_invoice_viewing_token_expiry.sql:41`). Soft-deleted proposals/invoices remain customer-readable + signable until the token expires. Combined with B4/B5 (`get_public_tenant_config`/`get_rep_contact` returning the first tenant_config row + first matching team_member globally — `supabase/migrations/20260429180000_add_public_rpc_functions.sql:18,34`), a multi-tenant deploy leaks branding + rep PII across tenants. Fix: add `AND deleted_at IS NULL` to every anon viewing/signing-token policy and `mark_proposal_signed`, AND make both public RPCs take `p_token` and resolve `tenant_id` through the proposal/invoice row.
