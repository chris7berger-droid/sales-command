## Tier definitions

The `Tier` column replaces the prior H/M/L Pri field. Tier captures
severity × likelihood-soon × cost × strategic leverage as one decision:

- **T0** — Drop everything. Active prod breakage, in-flight security incident.
- **T1** — This session. High-severity-and-likely × low-cost. Quick wins on customer-facing surfaces.
- **T2** — This sprint. High strategic leverage, or unblocks T1/T2 work.
- **T3** — When convenient. Low-severity bugs, refactor, polish.
- **T4** — Only if forced. Items needing a re-trigger before they can move (missing artifacts, blocked on external).

Dependency chains are surfaced with `Blocks: <ID>` / `Blocked by: <ID>`
as the first line of the Notes column.
