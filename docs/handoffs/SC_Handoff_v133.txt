DECISIONS / CHOICES MADE
─────────────────────────
- Picked Payment Links (Option A) over branded viewing-token landing
  page (Option B) after Chris's data: only ~20% of payments come
  through Stripe links, audience is residential + small GC (not
  large GCs who pay by check). Simpler is better for that crowd.

WEBHOOK SECRET REPAIR
─────────────────────
Root cause: SC_Handoff_v55 (2026-04-06) set up the SCC Stripe account
for the $699/mo Command Suite subscription billing on HDSP. New
secret `SCC_STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET` was added; in the process HDSP's
`STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET` got overwritten with the SCC value.

Until today, every `checkout.session.completed` delivery from the
HDSP webhook endpoint hit `verifyStripeSignature` → mismatch → 400
"Invalid signature". Stripe collected the money on Payment Intents,
but our invoice rows stayed `status='Sent'` and `qb-record-payment`
never fired. Customer-facing impact: status pill wrong in our app,
manual reconciliation in QB.

Fix: pulled the real HDSP whsec from Stripe Dashboard, ran
`supabase secrets set STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=<correct value>`.
Verified by clicking "Resend" on the failed 1:33 PM checkout.session.
completed → flipped to 200 → invoice #10010 marked Paid.

Backfill scope (CSV diagnostic):
- 46 Stripe charge rows since 2026-03-27
- 8 with Status='Paid' (rest were canceled = expired sessions, or
  requires_payment_method, or failed)
- Filtered out: 3 test invoices (#10010 today, two QB tests), 2
  pre-break invoices (#10000 Brian Royse 2026-04-04, already Paid in
  our DB from when the secret was correct), 2 Empire invoices
  (#90366, #90368) that aren't in the app's `invoices` table

- Net: 2 invoices needed DB backfill:
